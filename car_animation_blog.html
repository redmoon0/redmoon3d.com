Refining the Tunnel
  • Add Loop Cuts (Ctrl + R).
  • Duplicate (Shift + D) and separate (P) to make tunnel walls.
  • Extrude (E) and bevel (Ctrl + B) edges.

4. Adding Pillars & Barriers

Creating Pillars
  • Add a Cube and scale it into a pillar shape.
  • Place it at the tunnel entrance and apply an Array Modifier.
  • Assign a concrete material (use AmbientCG for free textures).
Adding Barriers
  • Extrude a cube into a barrier shape and bevel the edges.
  • Use Array and Mirror Modifiers to replicate the design.

5. Adding Road Signs & Lighting

Creating Arrow Signs
  • Add a plane, rotate and scale it.
  • Apply an arrow texture (find on Freepik or similar).
  • Use UV Editing to align the image.
  • Extrude the plane for thickness.
  • Use Array Modifier to duplicate.
Adding Lights
  • Create a small cube, bevel, and inset the faces.
  • Assign an Emission Shader to make it glow.
  • Duplicate lights along the tunnel roof or near signs.
